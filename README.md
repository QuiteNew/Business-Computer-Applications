# Business-Computer-Applications

This course covers the key concepts associated with office and business applications aspects. During the semester, the students will get the opportunity to learn the fundamentals of information technologies, including the most commonly used office applications for word processing and tabular computing. This course teaches and trains students to acquire skills for writing and editing different types of documents, making tabular calculations using proper function and formulas, sorting data by different criteria, creating charts from existing data, and designing interesting presentations. Besides that, students will get sufficient knowledge to incorporate the netiquette principles in business and academic communications.
Regarding the necessity for implementation of digital technologies in the increasingly dynamic business environment, to understand the basic concepts of new approaches to automate existing business processes taking advantage. In that sense, this course aims to familiarize students with related cutting-edge technologies. During the semester, students will assess, explore, analyze, and celebrate the treated areas, by mixing theory with practice. Furthermore, students will be challenged to apply concepts to real-world situations through training, exercises, and project assignments.
